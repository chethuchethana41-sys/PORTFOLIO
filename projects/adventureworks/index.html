Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing AdventureWorks sales data and transforming raw transactional information into meaningful business insights through an interactive Excel dashboard.

The dashboard enables business users, analysts, and decision-makers to monitor sales performance, identify trends, evaluate product performance, and understand customer behavior using clear visualizations and KPI indicators.

The objective was to simulate a real-world business intelligence scenario by applying data cleaning, transformation, analysis, and visualization techniques to support data-driven decision-making.

Dashboard Preview

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, revenue distribution, and key business metrics. Users can interact with filters to analyze performance across different categories, regions, and time periods.

Business Insights Generated

The analysis identified top-performing products contributing significantly to overall revenue, allowing businesses to prioritize high-value product segments.

Regional analysis highlighted variations in sales performance, helping stakeholders identify strong markets and areas requiring strategic improvement.

Trend analysis revealed seasonal sales patterns, enabling better demand forecasting and inventory planning.

Customer and product segmentation insights support targeted marketing strategies and optimized sales planning.

Tools and Technologies Used

Microsoft Excel for dashboard development and visualization

Power Query for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation

Data analysis techniques for KPI calculation and performance evaluation

Business Intelligence principles for dashboard design and insight generation
